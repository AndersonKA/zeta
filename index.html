Role Summary:

We have a great entry-level position for talented recent college grads with 0 - 2 years work experience that are looking for a great opportunity within a marketing services organization. If you have an interest in a fast-paced, production-oriented job, this may be right for you. Campaign Developer is responsible for building, editing and testing of email campaigns.

Principal Duties and Responsibilities:
  • Restructuring and/or hand-coding HTML files if client files do not meet design standards
  • Conversion of full Photoshop documents to HTML files
  • Act as HTML and email package expert for clients and account team(s)
  • Work with pre-existing or client-provided HTML files and provide simple design and image editing; make suggestions for coding improvements as needed
  • Coordinate email content (e.g. images, tracking codes, etc.) and change requests with account team and clients
  • Consult and troubleshoot on HTML/text templates, personalization, and URL/redirector issues
  • Conduct quality assurance checks to ensure the final product meets client expectations
  • Photoshop - crop, slice, minor image editing
  • Not a design position
Requirements:
  • 0-2 years experience
  • Expert knowledge of HTML and intermediate knowledge of hand-coding with table structures
  • Excellent attention to detail
  • Ability to meet tight deadlines using strong multitasking and organizational skills
  • Effectively problem solve in individual and in group settings
  • Team player who is comfortable with self-direction
  • Experience with Adobe Dreamweaver and Photoshop
  • Knowledge of design concepts & CSS
Preferred Skills:
  • Knowledge of server-side JavaScript
  • Experience with responsive HTML coding
  • Experience with other email deployment tools (ie: Exact Target, Responsys, Cheetah Mail)
